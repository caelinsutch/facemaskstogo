Anti-Microbial Reusable Mask

This is information regarding our Ani-microbial reusable face mask that can be used up to 20 times. The mask is made with (2 ply's) of Anti-microbial soft cotton/spandex, 2nd ply is the same material. This new mask adds an extra layer of protection for the user.

Safe and Reusable

Each mask can be reusable up to 20 times. Care Instructions: It is recommended that KOCO® masks be hand laundered using a non-allergenic laundry detergent, then air dried before wearing. Do NOT using dishwashing detergent, as many contain natural or synthetic bleach that can fade the mask and possibly negate the mask's antimicrobial defense. To ensure that the antimicrobial integrity of the mask is preserved, do NOT dry clean, and avoid the use of chlorine bleaches.
